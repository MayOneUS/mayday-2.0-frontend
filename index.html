We're going to end corruption, and we need your help.

Over 90% of Americans agree that big money in politics has broken our government. Over two hundred Members of Congress are already committed to reform. It’s time for us to finish the job.

We will pass reform by winning votes one-by-one.

We have strategically chosen a select number of representatives to focus on, and will run highly targeted campaigns in their districts urging them to join us. When we win them over, we will move on to others. Slowly but surely, we will tip the scales and pass reform to take back our democracy.

Our techniques are experimental

As our campaign evolves, we’ll measure how representatives respond. We’ll double-down on what works, and let go of what doesn’t. We’ll get smarter as we go. Our data-driven campaign has no place for ideological blinders. Mayday is in it to win it.

We can’t do this without you

Our plan’s success relies on the support of our community. We are counting on you to be part of the movement to restore the principles our republic was founded on and end the systemic corruption in Washington, D.C.
